FAQ'S

How does the AI personalize learning? +

TeachHub's AI analyzes your progress, identifies knowledge gaps, and dynamically adjusts the curriculum, content, and practice exercises to perfectly match your individual needs and learning speed.

What features are available for Tutors? +

Tutor Mode offers AI tools for automated grading, scheduling assistance, content generation, and deep insights into student performance, freeing up time for direct student engagement and mentorship.

Is TeachHub free to use? +

We offer a generous free tier for basic learning and teaching tools. Premium features, including advanced analytics and specialized AI tutoring modules, are available through subscription plans.

What subjects does TeachHub cover? +

TeachHub supports a wide range of subjects, from core STEM fields (Math, Science, Coding) to Humanities and Languages, with new AI content being added monthly.
